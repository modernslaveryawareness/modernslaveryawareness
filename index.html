What?

Modern Slavery refers to a range of exploitative practices that deprive individuals of their freedom, exploit them for labor or services, and often involve various forms of coercion, deception, or abuse, affecting approx. 49,600,000 individuals all over the world.

Why?

Vulnerable people, particularly women and children, are prone to being tricked, trapped, and exploited, e.g forced & early marriages, human trafficking, debt bondage, descent-based slavery, etc.

What have we done?

After conducting a survey at our school, we found that there were many students at our school who were not aware of the issue of Modern Slavery, despite it happening mere kilometres away from them. Hence, we concluded that it was imperative to inform our fellow students about the issue.
